An unconventional career ...

I nearly always worked at the fringe of corporations. These are the places nobody is proud of. These are the places seasoned employees are away because the risk/reward ratio is too high or the needed compensation frightens the employer.

This leaves a spot for younger candidate, a spot I was glad to take.

... which paid off

I worked de facto as an inhouse interim manager. This allowed me to become

  • financial manager at the age of 24
  • managing director at the age of 28
gaining experiences and experience at a very fast past.
